Welcome!

Private Flights

Our Private Flights aviation service is simple, (cost) efficient and the perfect way to travel across Europe – whether for business or pleasure. As co-owner all you need to do is give us a call and within 24 hours.

Aircraft Maintenance

Our experienced and highly-trained maintenance and repair crew are licensed by the FAA and EASA, authorising us to perform various legally-required inspections for a wide range of aircraft.

Airline Pilot Training

All LindSky flight training courses are approved by the European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) and the Belgium Civil Aviation Administration . However, we believe flight and pilot training is about more than meeting the minimum requirements of a qualification.

Whether you are looking for a place to park your aircraft for the short term or are in need of permanent hangar space, LindSky are able to meet your needs.
